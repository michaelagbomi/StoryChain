# StoryChain

A decentralized narrative writing and story exchange platform for sharing creative literature on Stacks blockchain.

## Features

- Story publishing with comprehensive narrative content
- Author word count management and validation
- Genre classification system
- Maturity rating specification and tracking
- Story publication management and unpublishing controls

## Smart Contract Functions

### Public Functions
- `publish-story` - Publish new narrative story with content
- `unpublish-story` - Unpublish story from platform (author only)

### Read-Only Functions
- `get-story` - Get narrative story details
- `get-author` - Get story author information
- `get-total-stories` - Get total published stories
- `get-publication-status` - Get story publication status

## Genres
- Fiction, Mystery, Romance, Fantasy, SciFi, Horror

## Maturity Ratings
- General, Teen, Young Adult, Adult, Mature

## Usage

Deploy the contract to create a literary platform where authors can publish their stories and manage publication status throughout the writing community.
